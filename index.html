Welcome, I'm Dr. Rohit Thanki

I am an AI Researcher and MedTech Consultant with over 15+ years of research experience and 5+ years in AI-driven MedTech startups across Europe, the Middle East, and India. As a AI & Computer Vision Expert, I specialize in developing cutting-edge solutions in healthcare AI, computer vision, medical imaging analysis, internet of medical things (IOMT), ownership authentication and copyright protection, especially in regulated environments involving Software as a Medical Device (SaMD).

With 20+ technical books and 50+ published research articles, I have been recognized among the top 2% of global research scientists by Stanford and Elsevier in 2024. My goal is to continue bridging the gap between academic innovation and commercial healthcare transformation, empowering both startups and healthcare providers to harness the full potential of artificial intelligence.
