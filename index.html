Welcome, I'm Dr. Rohit Thanki

I am a Senior AI Researcher and Data Scientist with over 15 years of scientific research experience and 8+ years in AI-powered MedTech innovation, working across Europe, the Middle East, and India. My expertise lies at the intersection of AI, medical imaging, and healthcare innovation, with a focus on developing advanced solutions in:

  • Healthcare AI & Clinical Decision Support
  • Computer Vision & Medical Image Analysis
  • Internet of Medical Things (IoMT)
  • Biometric Security & Ownership Authentication
  • Regulatory-Compliant Software as a Medical Device (SaMD)

I have successfully led end-to-end development of AI-powered diagnostic tools for diseases such as breast cancer, cervical cancer, oral cancer, skin cancer, brain tumors, and orthopaedic disorders, aligning with regulatory standards such as ISO 13485, CE marking, and FDA.

In addition to my industry work, I am a passionate academic with a strong publication record, having:

  • Authored 20+ technical books on AI in healthcare and biomedical engineering
  • Published 100+ peer-reviewed research articles indexed in Scopus and Web of Science
  • Been recognized in Stanford & Elsevier's Top 2% Global Scientists list (2024)

I aim to bridge the gap between academic innovation and real-world healthcare transformation, empowering startups, research institutions, and clinical stakeholders to harness AI for accessible, accurate, and impactful medical technologies.
