Welcome, I'm Dr. Rohit Thanki

My expertise lies at the intersection of AI, medical imaging, regulatory compliance, and healthcare innovation, with a focus on developing advanced solutions in:

  • Healthcare AI & Clinical Decision Support
  • Medical Imaging & Multimodal Data Analysis
  • MDR 2017/745, CE Marking & ISO 13485 Compliance
  • Quality Management Systems & Regulatory Documentation
  • Regulatory-Compliant Software as a Medical Device (SaMD)

I aim to bridge the gap between academic innovation and regulatory-compliant real-world healthcare transformation, empowering startups, MedTech companies, and clinical stakeholders to deliver safe, accessible, and impactful medical technologies worldwide.
